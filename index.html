Our Story

It Started With a Dead Bonsai

Our founder spent years killing every plant he touched. Succulents. Herbs. A cactus — yes, a cactus. But the dream of growing a tiny bonsai tree on his desk never went away.

So he obsessed. He tested every soil, every seed, every growing method — not to become a master gardener, but to find the simplest way for anyone to succeed.

The result was Avergo's first Bonsai Growing Kit: everything you need, nothing you don't, and instructions that actually make sense.

That kit became the #1 best-seller on Amazon. Today, over 2 million people have grown their first living thing with Avergo.
