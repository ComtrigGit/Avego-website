Our story

Started with a dying bonsai and a stubborn belief.

Leila Nasser bought her first bonsai on a whim in 2019. It died in three weeks. But instead of giving up, she became obsessed — why was growing something so beautiful also so unnecessarily hard?

Two years later, after testing hundreds of soil blends, pots, light setups, and seed varieties from her apartment in Amsterdam, Avergo was born. The mission was simple: make it so effortless and beautiful that anyone — even someone who has killed every plant they've ever owned — can succeed.

Today, Avergo kits have found homes in 40+ countries. Every product is designed with the same question Leila started with: what would make this impossible to fail?
